About The Project

Placebo Futures is a gallery of positive futures. We live in a time where people worry about what the future might hold for them. The world and the news around us depicts a dark vivid dystopia. Placebo Futures wants to change this picture where people together through creativity move towards a brighter tomorrow.

Placebo Futures is an online gallery of possible utopias, where everyone can upload and share their views of a positive future using stories, images and videos.

Help us imagine a better future and together we can heal these grim predictions. It does not matter how imaginative or creative your ideas might be, it is all about imagining a positive future for all of us. Upload and add your positive spin on a brighter future together with people from all around the world.
